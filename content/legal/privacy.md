# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Ember & Ash Coffee ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information about you when you use our website and purchase our products.

## What we collect

When you place an order, we collect your name, email address, delivery address, and payment information. Payment data is processed securely by our payment provider and we do not store card details.

When you sign up for our newsletter, we collect your email address only.

When you visit our website, we collect standard log data including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This helps us keep the site running and understand general usage patterns.

## How we use it

We use your order information to fulfil your purchase, communicate about your order, and send you a roast-date confirmation when your coffee ships.

We use your email address to send newsletters only if you have explicitly opted in. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.

We do not sell your data to third parties.

## Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep the site functioning and, with your consent, analytics cookies to understand how visitors use the site. See our [Cookie Policy](/legal/cookies) for details.

## Data retention

We retain order records for seven years for accounting and legal compliance. Newsletter subscriptions are retained until you unsubscribe. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.

## Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us by email and we will respond within 30 days.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please write to us at the email address listed on your order confirmation.
