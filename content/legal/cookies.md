# Cookie Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

This Cookie Policy explains how Ember & Ash Coffee uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how you use it.

## Cookies we use

**Essential cookies** are required for the site to function. They handle your shopping cart, session management, and checkout flow. These cannot be disabled without breaking the site.

**Analytics cookies** help us understand how visitors use the site — which pages are most useful, how long people stay, and where they come from. We use this to improve the site. These cookies are only set with your consent.

We do not use advertising cookies or share cookie data with advertising networks.

## Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to view, block, and delete cookies. Note that blocking essential cookies will prevent the cart and checkout from working.

You can also update your consent preferences at any time using the cookie settings link in the footer.

## Third-party services

We use a payment provider to process transactions securely. Their cookies are essential to completing checkout. See your payment confirmation for the provider's privacy policy.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as we add or remove services. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last revised.

## Contact

Questions about cookies? Email us at the address on your order confirmation.
