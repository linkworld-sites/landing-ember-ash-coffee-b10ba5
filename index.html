From Seed to Cup

The Ember & Ash Method

Four obsessive steps between a cherry on a hillside and the cup in your hands.

🌿

Origin Selection

We travel to farms at harvest, taste alongside producers, and only commit to lots that score 85+ on our SCA cupping sheets.

🚢

Direct Import

Green beans arrive in grain-pro sacks, stored in our climate-controlled warehouse to preserve peak freshness until roast day.

🔥

Precision Roasting

Each origin gets a tailored roast profile developed over dozens of test batches — every charge weight, preheat, and rate of rise mapped precisely.

📬

Roast & Ship

Your order triggers a fresh roast. Beans rest for 24 hours to degas, then ship in valve-sealed bags so freshness is locked until you open them.
